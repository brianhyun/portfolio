Questions and Answers with Brian Hyun

You lack experience.
Why should I hire you?

I assume you’ve taken a look at my projects. But, I guess you need a bit more convincing.

Hmm...I thought about it, and I’m not sure how to convince you. If my projects don’t convince you of my capabilities, then I don’t know what will. Everything else would be rhetoric, no?

Who are you really?

I’m someone trying to find life’s meaning through programming. It has a lot to teach in terms of failure-tolerance, something I’ve always struggled with. However, my pain-tolerance makes me a bit insane in the head, so I’ve never really known when to give up.

Why web development?
And, why front-end specifically?

Web development is immediately gratifying. There’s nothing like writing code and seeing the results of your creation in an ‘alt-tab, cmd+r.’

Front-end is where visual design meets programming and, since I like both, that’s where I want to be. But, I’m not deaf to Backend since that’s my current end goal: becoming a Full Stack Developer. But, I’m in no particular rush and taking it one step at a time seems wise.

Any hobbies?

Outside of programming, I like bouldering and taking photos.

Life motivation?

Becoming someone I can be proud of.

Doing something for this world.
